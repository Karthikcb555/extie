Welcome to Extia!

Since its creation in 2007, Extia, a Consulting company specializing in IT, Engineering, and Digital professions, has favoured an approach that combines performance and well-being at work. Such vision of the company is shared today by more than 2 500 Extians in France and abroad, and rewarded since 2012 by the Great Place to Work® label. First who, then what. Discover the positive experience by Extia.

First who,

First who,

In an ever-changing world, it is up to individuals to find simple and effective solutions to adjust to reality. This observation is our strategy: to surround ourselves with the right people and trust them to jointly build a meaningful professional project. Such intrapreneurial principle has been with us since our creation and has contributed to the realization of all our great stories.

Consulting & Transformation
Technology is a means, not an end. The means for our clients to offer new services, imagine other uses, and create more value for their stakeholders. Extia supports you in the management of your transformation projects, by establishing the link between people and digital. In a resolutely agile approach. And sometimes disruptive.

It & digital
No need to make a long speech on the opportunities that digital technology has to offer. It is still necessary to properly understand the challenges and transform these opportunities into value creation. Our mission? To make simple what is complex, optimize your performance, and bring your IT & Digital projects to life. Our sectoral and geographic coverage allows us to be as close as possible to your needs, and to cover the entire IS value chain. With a touch (or two) of agility.
