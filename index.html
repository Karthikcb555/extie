Welcome to Extia Canada!

Since its creation in 2007, Extia, a Consulting company specializing in IT, Engineering, and Digital professions, has favoured an approach that combines performance and well-being at work. Such vision of the company is shared today by more than 2500 Extians in France and abroad, and rewarded since 2012 by the Great Place to Work® label. First who, then what.
